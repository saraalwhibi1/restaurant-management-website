Welcome to Manage Me

Our Story

Manage Me, founded by Sara Alwhibi, Raghad Alshamrani, Ghadah Altuwaim and Najd Alnasrallah in 2021, is an industry leading restaurant management platform. We provide table reservations managment, staff rating mechanism and orders managment to restaurants to increase efficiency and revenue.

Our Mission & Vision

With our passion for hospitality, we take pride in bringing together people and the restaurants they manage.
